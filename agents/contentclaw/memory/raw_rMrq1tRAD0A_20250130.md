## Raw Content - Menlo Ventures State of Generative AI in the Enterprise 2025

Key Insights:
1. Enterprises spent $37 billion on generative AI in 2025, up from $11.5 billion in 2024 (3.2x growth)
2. 76% of AI use cases are purchased rather than built internally (vs 53% in 2024)
3. AI buyers convert at 47% vs 25% for traditional SaaS - nearly 2x higher conversion
4. 27% of AI application spend comes through PLG motions - 4x the rate of traditional software
5. Startups capture 63% of AI application market ($19B), up from 36% last year
6. Coding is the killer use case - $4B spend (55% of departmental AI)
7. Anthropic now leads enterprise LLM market with 40% share (OpenAI at 27%)
8. Healthcare leads vertical AI adoption at $1.5B (43% of vertical AI spend)
9. Only 16% of enterprise deployments are true agents - most are simple workflows
10. AI is now 6% of entire global SaaS market after just 3 years
